I paint about time — the kind that passes whether or not anyone is watching. Still lifes, abstract figures, and above all the minute in which ink, dropped into water, stops obeying the hand that dropped it.

Much of the work begins as a provocation: a single gesture whose consequences are allowed to continue without me. What remains on the paper is a record of that negotiation between intention and surrender.

This site is a studio made public, not an institution. Nothing here claims completion.
